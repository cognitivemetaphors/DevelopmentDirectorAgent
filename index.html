Curriculum

St. Anthony DLC offers a curriculum aligned with the Department of Education Standards to children in Junior Kinder and Senior Kinder. Through a creative play approach, students' interests and needs are met to help them develop their full potentials.

Commitment to the Community

St. Anthony DLC provides free quality education to underprivileged children of Cutcut Guiguinto Bulacan. Every year, there are at least 20 students or more who benefit from this free quality Kinder education from the school.

The school offers a very minimal amount of school fees to those children whose families cannot afford to send them to private schools due to high tuition fees. The family pays an amount proportional to their income - as low as Php 5,000.00 compared with Php 15,000.00 at nearby schools.
